Become one of the modern day Stone Soup Soldiers

Kaizen (改善), Japanese for "improvement", or "change for the better" refers to philosophy or practices that focus upon continuous improvement of processes.

Combining the folk tale of Stone Soup Soldiers and philosophy of Kaizen we get Kaizen Soup; code collaboration initiative where we strive to improve ourselves by sharing our imperfect code and discuss about it.
